## Executive Summary

This page contains valuable information for humans, but **AI systems are likely to struggle extracting a clean, reusable answer** from it.

The main issues are not related to keywords or rankings.  
They relate to **structure, directness, and semantic completeness**.

**Overall AI Visibility (estimated):** ⚠️ Medium–Low  
**Main risk:** Content is readable but not *answer-ready*

---

## 1. Prompt Bypass Rate  
*Can an LLM extract a clear answer from imperfect prompts?*

**Score:** 42 / 100  

### Observations
- Core answer is **spread across multiple paragraphs**
- Key definitions are implied, not stated explicitly
- Several sentences require context from previous sections

### Example
When prompted with:
> “Explain what this page is about in 2–3 sentences”

The model produces:
- a vague summary  
- no reusable definition  
- no quotable answer block  

### Why this matters
AI search systems rarely use *perfect prompts*.  
If your content requires ideal wording to be understood, **it will be skipped**.

---

## 2. Tone Match  
*Does the content match AI answer-style tone?*

**Score:** 58 / 100  

### Strengths
- Neutral language
- No excessive marketing claims
- Generally coherent flow

### Gaps
- Overuse of narrative framing (“let’s explore”, “you might wonder”)
- Few declarative, standalone statements
- Long sentences with multiple ideas

### AI preference
AI systems favor:
- short declarative sentences  
- explicit definitions  
- answer-first phrasing  

---

## 3. Structural Clarity  
*How easy is it for AI to segment the content?*

**Score:** 51 / 100  

### Findings
- Headings are thematic, not functional  
- Several sections contain multiple unrelated ideas  
- No dedicated “answer blocks” or summary sections  

### Missing elements
- Direct answers under headings  
- Lists instead of paragraphs  
- Clear hierarchy (H2 → H3 → bullets)

---

## 4. Semantic Gap Radar  
*What AI expects but doesn’t find*

**Critical gaps (High priority)**
- Explicit definition of the core concept
- Clear differentiation from adjacent concepts
- One-paragraph “how it works” explanation

**Medium priority gaps**
- Practical example or use case
- Limitations / edge cases
- Short FAQ-style clarifications

**Low priority gaps**
- Terminology consistency
- Internal cross-references

## What Would Improve This Page Most

Top 3 high-impact changes:

1. Add a **clear, 3–4 sentence answer block** near the top  
2. Rewrite key sections into **definition-first format**  
3. Introduce **explicit subtopics AI expects**, even briefly  

These changes do **not** require rewriting the whole page.
